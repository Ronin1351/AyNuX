About Us

AyNuX is the provider of quality, performance and efficient solution to any size IT infrastructure. By inspiring IT to focus on the applications and services; AyNuX solutions merges web-scale engineering and consumer-grade design to a unique and adaptable converge platform, storage, and networking, into a resilient software-defined solution that power their company or business.
